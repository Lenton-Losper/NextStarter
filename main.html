How It Works

Our platform streamlines the co-founder matching process from start to finish. Founders begin by creating detailed profiles of their company, prototype, or concept, including specific co-founder requirements. Our skill-based matching system then connects founders with potential partners who have relevant experience and complementary skills. Before making any commitments, users can view each other's portfolio of past projects and engage through our pitch system. We encourage trial collaborations on small projects to ensure compatibility. For early-stage founders, our platform offers tools to showcase concept designs and build waiting lists to generate interest. Additionally, startups can utilize our equity-based fundraising feature to secure investment directly through the platform.
